Our Story

Light That Preserves Moments

How it began

It started with a single pour on a kitchen table.

Aura Archives began the way most good things do — quietly, and a little by accident. One winter, between long workdays, we melted down a pot of soy wax just to see if we could make the house smell like the calm we couldn't find anywhere else.

The first candle was lopsided. The second tunnelled. By the nineteenth, the studio fell quiet and someone simply said, "oh." That was the one. That was the moment a hobby became a promise.

We named it Aura Archives because we'd come to believe a scent can archive a feeling — store it the way a photograph stores light, and hand it back the instant you strike a match.
