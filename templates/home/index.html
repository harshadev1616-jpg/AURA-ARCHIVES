Our Story

Every Candle is a
Preserved Moment

Aura Archives was born from a love of fragrance, ritual, and the quiet magic of candlelight. Each candle is hand-poured in small batches using 100% natural soy wax, premium cotton wicks, and carefully curated fragrance blends.

We believe that scent has the power to transport you — to a memory, a feeling, a place. That is why every Aura Archives candle is crafted to preserve moments.
